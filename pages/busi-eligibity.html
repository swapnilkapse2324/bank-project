Punjab Business Loan : Eligibility

1. Age Criteria

  • Min. 21 years at the time of loan application & Max. 65 years at the time of loan maturity .

    • 2.Eligible Entities

  • Individuals, Startups, MSMEs, Sole Proprietorships, Partnership Firms, Public and Private Limited Companies, Limited Liability Partnerships, retailers, traders, manufacturers, and other non-farm income-generating business entities engaged only in the services, trading, and manufacturing sectors

    • 3. Business Vintage

  • Min. 1 year or above

    • 4. Business experience

  • Min. 1 year, business location to remain same

    • 5.Annual Turnover

  • Shall be defined by the Bank/NBFC

    • 6. Credit Score

  • 750 or above (Preferred by most private and public sector banks)

    • 7. Nationality

  • Indian citizens, should not have defaulted on any previous loan(s) with any lender

    • 8. Additional Criteria

  • Applicant must own either a residence, office, shop, or godown
